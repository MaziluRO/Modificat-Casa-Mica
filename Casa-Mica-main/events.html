Private Events

If it wasn't enough to have 10 accommodation rooms, a restaurant, a swimming pool, a swing and a cat, we also have two spacious rooms where you can organize events.

While our restaurant is closed, it can still be used to organize private or corporate feasts, all within out 40 seats limit. You can rent our restaurant for private events like: baptisms, weddings, birthdays.

The library is not just a simple activity room, but rather a space for personal development. Development can be on any level, in any field, depending on what you want.

The library comes equiped with:

  • Air condition
  • Video projector + projection screen
  • Flipchart
  • Audio system
  • Wireless internet
