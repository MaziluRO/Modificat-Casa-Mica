About Us

Hello! We are the Moldoveanu family and we want to talk a little about ourselves.

We love animals. We have a black cat named Miti. We had a parrot and a turtoise named Flopi who was very sociable and liked to come at the living room table whenever we had guests.

The idea of boarding tourism is old! In 1987, while walking through the Iezer - Papusa massif, we found a deserted forest hut somewhere right at the entrance to the Riusor valley. With the help of a kind mountain rescuer, we got to know the head of the forest district from Cimpulung Muscel. Using the Union and the Tourism Club from the Oncological Institute where we were working together with Pic, my wife, we achieved something we did not really hoped for: we got entrusted with the cottage in custody.

Our History

In the '90s we managed to buy what the locals told us was a good place to stay. Now we have a house and a half-hectare plot of land where you can't leave. A boarding house also appears there. After the Revolution we enter the roller coaster of different businesses ... Better, less good ... Since '96 we have been struggling with a warehouse of construction materials that was missing on the way back and forth. Standing in the yard, I had a property of 2 houses. In the small house, a 2-room apartment where I had offices. Things were going so well that we started flirting with the idea of ​​emptying the offices, renovating them and renting them in a hotel system.

It was a major change from what I had done for the last 15 years. We were coding. Mother had also heard of this idea. And she didn't code. Leaving on vacation in June 2003, he simply took the workers from the company, moved our offices to the living room and began renovating the Small House. When we returned, I had taken the shot in the ass and was on the slide. At the end of August, my house was renovated and ... empty. We had run out of money. We had just told our friends and one of them, Dan, called us: Do you have a vacant house? "Very free" - we say that we knew the wind was blowing ... We find ourselves in half an hour with him at the gate with some customers. They had to stay in Bucharest for a month, they were staying at the Ambassador Hotel and they felt so good that they started looking elsewhere. After entering the yard, they asked us if they could bring their things that afternoon. Pic and I looked at each other and thought we had lost the first train ...

We were not a little surprised when, finding out how free the house is, we spent 48 hours furnishing. He borrows money, runs after furniture, TV, etc., etc. and in the allotted time I had a fully equipped room. Two days after they settled in, they called other colleagues ... The Bulgarians had begun to roll. Every month more customers, a new body put into use .... We started to taste our little success: the satisfaction of satisfied people, who feed our account with a smile on their face!
